Jitter Bug Centroid Plotting Tool

This lightweight, client-side tool lets you quickly visualize the Centroid X and Centroid Y data coming out of your Jitter Bug measurements. Simply drag & drop (or click to browse) your CSV file anywhere on the page and watch your points render in an interactive Plotly scatter plot. When you’re happy with the result, hit “Download Plot as PNG” to save the image.
